Fear Tracker

Fear Tracker is a tool where you can add records of your fear level and plot to see how your fear has changed. It is suggested that records should be recorded every day at the same time. Tool is very useful for monitoring fear level during pharmacotherapy for Anxiety disorders like: Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Social anxiety disorder, Obsessive-compulsive disorder.
